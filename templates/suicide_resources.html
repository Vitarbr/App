Suicide Resources

Here are some suicide resources. If you are considering suicide, please give these a chance. Whenever possible, however, seek assistance from a therapist. You can find one in your local area, or even on here, if you like.

National Suicide Hotline

800-273-2855

This national suicide hotline is open 24/7, and gaurentees to be free and confidential. You will connect with a real person here. Again, this is completely confidential, so you shouldn't feel nervous talking to them. They're here to help.

Do you know someone who might be at risk?
  1. Ask.
  2. Keep them safe.
  3. Be there.
  4. Help them connect.
  5. Follow up.

The list above was taken from "Be The 1 To", a site dedicated making everyone capable of dealing with a crisis suicide situation with their five-step method. I highly recommend you check them out- they have posters and such, which would be amazing if they were distributed in work/school.
